Rodrigues's Rotation Formula

by Chris Tralie

Recall that the formula to rotate a vector v around an axis k by an angle of θ is

\[ \cos(\theta) \vec{v} + (1 - \cos(\theta))(\vec{k} \cdot \vec{v}) \vec{k} + \sin(\theta)(\vec{k} \times \vec{v}) \]

Fill in the method rodriguesRot in anglefunctions.js to implement this, using functions in the glMatrix library. To test it, input an axis, angle, and vector in the menu to the right, and click doRotation. The results will be displayed in plotly
