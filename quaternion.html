Quaternion Rotation Formula

by Chris Tralie

Recall that to rotate a vector v = (x, y, z) around a unit axis k = (a, b, c) by an angle of θ, one constructs the quaternions

\[ q = e^{\frac{\theta}{2}(ai + bj + ck)} = \cos\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right) + ia \sin\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right) + jb \left(\sin\frac{\theta}{2}\right) + kc \sin\left(\frac{\theta}{2}\right)\]

and then

\[ p = xi + yj + zk \]

then the components of the multiplication

\[ q p q^{-1} \]

Give the results of the rotation
